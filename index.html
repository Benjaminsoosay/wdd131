Wetaskiwin, Alberta

Wetaskiwin is a city in Alberta, Canada, located approximately 70 kilometres south of the provincial capital of Edmonton. The city name comes from the Cree word wītaskiwinihk, meaning "the hills where peace was made".

Wetaskiwin is home to Canada's oldest operating gas station and the Reynolds-Alberta Museum, which preserves and celebrates Alberta's mechanical heritage. The city is surrounded by picturesque prairie landscape and offers a peaceful, family-friendly environment.
